Huimin Zheng · Professor / Postdoctoral Fellow

Professor at Langfang Normal University, Postdoctoral researcher at Tianjin University, recipient of the National Excellent Postdoctoral Fellow award. Research covers molecular simulation & adsorption mechanisms, agronomy and molecular breeding.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering, China University of Petroleum (Beijing), 2009–2015
  • B.Eng., Qufu Normal University, 2005–2009
  • Visiting Scholar, California State University, Long Beach, 2018–2020

Professional Experience

  • Professor, Langfang Normal University, 2024–present
  • Associate Professor, Langfang Normal University, 2018–2024
  • Postdoctoral Fellow / Senior Bioinformatics Engineer, Tianjin University × Novogene, 2023–present

Research Areas

Molecular simulation of adsorption/diffusion in porous materials, VOC removal, fuel desulfurization; molecular breeding & genotyping chips applied to rice, cotton, citrus.

Selected Publications

  2. Zheng H.*, Unlocking selective desulfurization: HY zeolite, Microporous and Mesoporous Materials, 2025.
  3. Zheng H., Adsorption Mechanism of Benzene Alkylation, Langmuir, 2022.
